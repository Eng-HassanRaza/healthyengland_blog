Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. To make a request, contact us at privacy@healthyengland.com.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with the updated date.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@healthyengland.com or via our Contact page.
